Computer, does your character have/is your character a...


HOW TO PLAY:
Objective: To guess the computer's character
1 - Draw a card to get your character
2 - Select an attribute and hit CONTINUE... to make a guess
3 - The appropriate cards will be turned over
4 - When you are ready to guess your opponent's name,
do so in the box and hit SUBMIT NAME.
Warning! A wrong guess will cause you to lose the game!
